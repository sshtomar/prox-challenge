# Page 17: MIG Welding LCD Screen Setup Sequence

This page shows the LCD display screens for setting up MIG (solid core, gas shielded) welding, with screenshots of the actual welder interface at each step.

## Screen 1: Process Selection (Home Screen)
Screenshot shows the LCD with "MIG" highlighted in the process carousel at the top. Other process options visible: icons for different welding processes scrollable with the Main Control Knob. The VULCAN logo appears on the left, OMNIPRO 220 on the right. HOME and BACK buttons are labeled on the bezel.

Below the screen:
- **Left Knob** (marked with amperage symbol)
- **Main Control Knob** (large central knob) -- turn to scroll through processes, press to select
- **Right Knob** (marked with voltage symbol)

## Screen 2: Wire Type and Diameter Selection
After selecting MIG, the screen shows:
- **Wire Type** selection (left side): options include solid core wire types
- **Wire Diameter** (left knob): turn Left Knob to set wire diameter
- **Gas Type** (right side): shows the shielding gas selection

## Screen 3: Wire Diameter and Gas Settings
Screenshot shows settings screen with:
- Left Knob controls **Wire Diameter** selection
- Right Knob controls **Gas Type** selection
- CONFIRM option at the bottom to proceed

## Screen 4: Auto Weld Settings (Main Welding Screen)
This is the primary operating screen during MIG welding. Screenshot shows:
- **Top-left large number**: Output amperage/wire feed speed (adjustable with Left Knob)
- **Top-right large number**: Output voltage (adjustable with Right Knob)
- **Bottom-left**: Shows "0 A" (actual welding current, displays during welding)
- **Bottom-right**: Shows "0.0 V" (actual voltage, displays during welding)
- **Bottom status bar** shows: Process type, Wire type, Wire diameter, Gas type, and a SETTINGS shortcut

The Left Knob adjusts wire feed speed/amperage. The Right Knob adjusts voltage. The synergic system automatically links these for optimal settings based on the wire type and diameter selected.
