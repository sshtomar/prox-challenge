# Quick Start Guide - Page 2: Cable Setup for All 4 Welding Processes

This page shows four separate cable setup diagrams, one for each welding process. Each diagram shows the rear/bottom of the welder with labeled cable connections and numbered steps.

## 1. STICK CABLE SETUP (Upper-Left)

Diagram shows the welder from the rear/bottom angle with two cables:
- **Step 1:** Connect ground clamp to **negative terminal** (left socket, marked -)
- **Step 2:** Connect electrode holder to **positive terminal** (right socket, marked +)
- **Step 3:** Leave wire feed power disconnected
- Numbered callouts in diagram: (1) points to electrode holder cable in positive socket, (2) points to ground clamp cable area, (3) points to the disconnected wire feed area

## 2. MIG CABLE SETUP (Lower-Left)

Diagram shows the welder with MIG gun and cables:
- **Step 1:** Connect ground clamp to **negative terminal** (left socket, marked -)
- **Step 2:** Connect wire feed power to **positive terminal** (right socket, marked +)
- **Step 3:** Connect MIG gun to wire feed (internal connection)
- **Step 4:** Connect wire feed control inside welder
- Numbered callouts: (1) ground clamp to negative, (2) wire feed power to positive, (3) MIG gun connection, (4) wire feed control cable

## 3. FLUX CABLE SETUP (Lower-Center, boxed)

Text box states: "Follow MIG cable setup but connect ground clamp to positive terminal and wire feed power to negative terminal."
- This is the REVERSE of MIG polarity:
  - Ground clamp -> **Positive** terminal
  - Wire feed power -> **Negative** terminal

## 4. TIG CABLE SETUP (Lower-Right)

Diagram shows the welder with TIG torch, foot pedal, and gas line:
- **Step 1:** Connect ground clamp to **positive terminal**
- **Step 2:** Connect TIG torch to **negative terminal**
- **Step 3:** Connect gas line to gas regulator
- **Step 4:** Connect foot pedal inside welder
- **Step 5:** Leave wire feed power disconnected
- Numbered callouts: (1) ground clamp, (2) TIG torch, (3) gas line, (4) foot pedal cable, (5) disconnected wire feed
