# Page 9: Interior Controls Diagram

A labeled photograph showing the inside of the Vulcan OmniPro 220 with the side door open, revealing the wire feed mechanism and internal controls.

## Components (labeled with callout arrows):

### Upper Area:
- **Cold Wire Feed Switch** -- located at the top center of the interior compartment. A small toggle or button used to feed wire through the gun cable without energizing the welder. Used during initial setup to thread wire through the liner and out the MIG gun nozzle.

### Wire Feed Mechanism (Center):
- **Wire Feed Mechanism** -- the central assembly containing the drive motor and feed rollers. This is where the welding wire passes through from the spool to the gun cable. Located in the middle of the interior compartment.
- **Feed Tensioner** -- located to the upper-right of the wire feed mechanism. A knob/lever assembly that controls how much pressure the feed rollers apply to the welding wire. Proper tension settings: 3-5 for solid wire, 2-3 for flux-cored wire.
- **Idler Arm** -- spring-loaded arm adjacent to the feed tensioner, to the right of the drive rollers. Swings up to release wire tension and down to engage. Must be latched across the tip of the arm to hold wire under tension.
- **Feed Roller Knob** -- knob that secures the feed roller in place. Unscrew counterclockwise to remove and flip/replace the feed roller.

### Left Side:
- **Wire Spool** -- mounted on the left side of the interior. The spool spindle accepts 1-2 lb spools directly, or 10-12 lb spools with the Spool Adapter.
- **Spool Knob** -- large black knob on the face of the wire spool. Secures the spool to the spindle (for 10-12 lb spools, screws into the Spool Adapter).

### Lower Area:
- **Wire Inlet Liner** -- the tube through which welding wire travels from the spool into the feed mechanism. The wire must be guided through here during setup.
- **Foot Pedal Socket** -- round multi-pin connector at the bottom-left of the interior. The optional foot pedal cable (sold separately, used for TIG welding) plugs in here. The cable routes through a hole in the welder front.
- **Wire Feed Control Socket** -- round multi-pin connector at the bottom-right of the interior, next to the foot pedal socket. The Wire Feed Control Cable from the MIG gun plugs in here. Note: the plug fits in only one specific orientation.
