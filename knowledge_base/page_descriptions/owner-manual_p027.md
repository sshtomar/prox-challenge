# Page 27: Stick Cable Setup + Power Cord Connection

## Upper Section: Stick Welding Cable Setup

### Title: "Stick Setup - Connect Cables"

Large labeled diagram showing the cable routing for Stick (SMAW) welding:

- **Ground Clamp Cable** connects to **Negative (-) Socket** -- cable runs from the left socket (negative) to the **Ground Clamp** which attaches to the workpiece. Text: "Ground Clamp Cable in Negative Socket."
- **Electrode Holder Cable** connects to **Positive (+) Socket** -- cable runs from the right socket (positive) to the **Electrode Holder**. Text: "Electrode Holder Cable in Positive Socket."
- The **Ground Clamp** is shown as a spring-loaded clamp at the end of the cable
- The **Electrode Holder** is shown as a spring-loaded jaw that holds the stick electrode

### Stick Polarity Summary:
- **Polarity: DCEP** (Direct Current Electrode Positive)
- Ground Clamp -> Negative (-) Socket
- Electrode Holder -> Positive (+) Socket
- This is the SAME polarity as MIG solid core (DCEP) but OPPOSITE of TIG and Flux-Cored (DCEN)

### Setup Steps:
1. Plug Ground Clamp Cable into Negative Socket. Twist clockwise all the way to lock in place.
2. Plug Electrode Holder Cable into Positive Socket. Twist clockwise all the way to lock in place.

**Note:** Leave wire feed power disconnected for Stick welding -- the wire feed mechanism is not used.

## Lower Section: Connect Power Cord

Diagram showing the rear/side of the welder with the power input:

- **Power Input** socket is labeled on the back/side of the welder
- Text: "Plug either 120VAC or 240VAC Power Cord into Power Input Socket."
- **Note:** Plug will only fit one way (keyed connection).
- **WARNING! TO PREVENT SERIOUS INJURY FROM ACCIDENTAL OPERATION:** Do not plug cord into wall outlet at this time.
- The diagram shows the welder from an angle with the Power Input socket visible and a power cord being inserted

## POLARITY QUICK REFERENCE (All Processes):
| Process | Polarity | Positive (+) Socket | Negative (-) Socket |
|---|---|---|---|
| MIG (Solid Core) | DCEP | Wire Feed Power Cable | Ground Clamp |
| Flux-Cored (Gasless) | DCEN | Ground Clamp | Wire Feed Power Cable |
| TIG | DCEN | Ground Clamp | TIG Torch |
| Stick | DCEP | Electrode Holder | Ground Clamp |
