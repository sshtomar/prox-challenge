# Page 24: TIG Cable Setup Diagram

Large labeled diagram showing all cable connections required for TIG welding on the Vulcan OmniPro 220.

## Cable Connections (shown with labeled arrows):

### Power Cables:
1. **Ground Clamp Cable** connects to **Positive (+) Socket** -- the Ground Clamp cable runs from the right socket (positive, marked +) on the bottom of the welder front to the Ground Clamp which attaches to the workpiece. Text: "Ground Clamp Cable in Positive Socket." Twist clockwise all the way to lock in place.

2. **TIG Torch Cable** connects to **Negative (-) Socket** -- the TIG Torch cable runs from the left socket (negative, marked -) on the bottom of the welder front. Text: "TIG Torch Cable in Negative Socket." Twist clockwise all the way to lock in place.

### Gas Connection:
3. **Shielding Gas Hose** -- runs from the TIG Torch to the gas regulator/cylinder (connection details on page 25). The hose is visible running from the TIG Torch assembly to the left side of the setup.

### Foot Pedal:
4. **Foot Pedal** (sold separately) -- shown as a flat pedal unit on the floor. The **Foot Pedal Cable** routes through a hole on the Welder front and connects to the **Foot Pedal Socket** (inside the welder). The connection point is labeled "Foot Pedal Socket (inside Welder)." Secure by turning collar clockwise until tight.

### Components Labeled in Diagram:
- **TIG Torch** (sold separately) -- shown as a hand-held torch with cable
- **Ground Clamp** -- spring-loaded clamp at the end of the ground cable
- **Foot Pedal** (sold separately) -- floor pedal for controlling amperage during TIG welding

## TIG Polarity Summary:
- **Polarity: DCEN** (Direct Current Electrode Negative)
- Ground Clamp -> Positive (+) Socket
- TIG Torch -> Negative (-) Socket
- This is the SAME polarity as Flux-Cored (DCEN) but OPPOSITE of MIG solid core and Stick (DCEP)

## Setup Steps Listed:
1. Plug Ground Clamp Cable into Positive Socket. Twist clockwise all the way to lock in place.
2. Plug TIG Torch Cable (TIG Torch sold separately) into Negative Socket. Twist clockwise all the way to lock in place.
3. Insert the Foot Pedal Cable (Foot Pedal sold separately) through hole on Welder front and connect to the Foot Pedal Socket inside the machine. Secure by turning collar clockwise until tight.
4. For connecting Shielding Gas Hose, refer to Connect Shielding Gas section on next page.
