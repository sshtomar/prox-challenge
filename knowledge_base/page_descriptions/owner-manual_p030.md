# Page 30: TIG Welding LCD Screen Setup Sequence

## TIG Welding Procedure Steps with LCD Screenshots:

### Screen 1: Process Selection
LCD shows "TIG" highlighted in the process carousel. Turn Main Control Knob to scroll until TIG appears, then press to select.

### Screen 2 (Step 9a): Polarity and Gas Settings
LCD displays the polarity confirmation screen:
- Shows Negative (-) and Positive (+) socket indicators with cable routing diagram
- Text: "100% Argon"
- Shows which cables connect where (matching the TIG polarity: Torch to negative, Ground to positive)
- Gas type confirmed as 100% Argon
- CONFIRM button at bottom
- Instructions: Plug cables in according to screen, connect gas according to screen, set SCFH between 10-25

### Screen 3 (Step 9b): Rod Diameter and Material Thickness
LCD displays:
- **Left side labeled "Diameter"**: Shows tungsten rod diameter (e.g., "1/16\""). Turn Left Knob to change.
- **Right side labeled "Thickness"**: Shows material thickness (e.g., "24 Ga"). Turn Right Knob to change.
- Title bar shows "TIG" process indicator
- CONFIRM button at bottom

### Screen 4 (Step 9c): Auto Weld Settings (Main TIG Welding Screen)
LCD displays the operating screen:
- **Top-left large number**: "30 A" -- set output amperage (adjustable with Left Knob)
- **Top-right**: "ON / OFF" toggle -- turn Right Knob to ON to energize TIG Torch
- **Bottom-left**: "0 A" -- actual welding current (displays during welding)
- **Bottom-right**: "0.0 V" -- actual voltage (displays during welding)
- **Bottom status bar**: Shows "TIG with Pedal", gas type "100% Ar", rod diameter "1/16\"", material thickness "24Ga", SETTINGS shortcut

**WARNING! TO PREVENT SERIOUS INJURY: Welder is now energized and Open Circuit Voltage is present** when Right Knob is turned to ON.

Labels with arrows point to:
- **Rod Diameter** indicator (bottom-left of status bar)
- **Material Thickness** indicator (bottom-right of status bar)
- **Process** indicator
- **Gas Type** indicator
