# Page 41: Maintenance Procedures

## MIG Gun Nozzle and Contact Tip Inspection and Cleaning

### Nozzle/Contact Tip Diagram:
Exploded view of the MIG Gun tip assembly showing:
- **Nozzle** -- the outer cylindrical cup that directs shielding gas around the arc. Pulls straight off to remove.
- **Contact Tip** -- the inner copper tip through which the welding wire passes. Unscrews counterclockwise from the gun body.
- The diagram shows the Nozzle and Contact Tip separated from the MIG Gun body, with labels pointing to each component.

### Cleaning Procedure:
1. Make sure the entire MIG Gun is completely cool and the Power Cord is unplugged
2. Pull the Nozzle to remove it
3. Scrub the interior of the Nozzle clean with a wire brush
4. Examine the end of the Nozzle -- should be flat and even. If uneven, chipped, melted, cracked, or damaged, replace it
5. Unscrew the Contact Tip counterclockwise and slide it off the welding wire to remove
6. Scrub the outside of the Tip clean with a wire brush. Clean out the inside with a tip cleaner (sold separately)
7. Examine the shape of the hole at the end of the Contact Tip -- should be an even circle with no oblong shape or bulges
8. If any problems, replace the Contact Tip with the correct size for the welding wire used
9. Reinstall the Tip and securely reinstall the Nozzle
