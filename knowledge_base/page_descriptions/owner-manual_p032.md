# Page 32: Stick Welding LCD Screen Setup Sequence

## Stick Welding Procedure Steps with LCD Screenshots:

### Screen 1: Process Selection
LCD shows "Stick" highlighted in the process carousel. Turn Main Control Knob to scroll until Stick appears, then press to select.

### Screen 2 (Step 7b): Electrode Type Selection
LCD displays:
- Title: "Stick" with "Electrode" subtitle
- Shows electrode type code: **"60xx"** (represents 60xx series electrodes like 6010, 6011, 6013)
- Turn Main Control Knob to cycle through electrode types (60xx, 70xx, etc.)
- CONFIRM button at bottom

### Screen 3 (Step 7a): Polarity Setting
LCD displays the polarity confirmation screen:
- Shows Negative (-) and Positive (+) socket indicators
- Text: "No Gas Required"
- Shows cable routing matching Stick polarity (Electrode Holder to positive, Ground Clamp to negative)
- CONFIRM button at bottom
- Instructions: Plug cables in according to screen

### Screen 4 (Step 7c): Electrode Diameter and Material Thickness
LCD displays:
- **Left side labeled "Diameter"**: Shows electrode diameter (e.g., "3/32\""). Turn Left Knob to change.
- **Right side labeled "Thickness"**: Shows material thickness (e.g., "16 Ga"). Turn Right Knob to change.
- Title bar shows "Stick" process indicator
- CONFIRM button at bottom

### Screen 5 (Step 7d): Auto Weld Settings (Main Stick Welding Screen)
LCD displays the operating screen:
- **Top-left large number**: "60 A" -- set output amperage (adjustable with Left Knob)
- **Top-right**: "ON / OFF" toggle -- turn Right Knob to ON to energize Electrode Holder
- **Bottom-left**: "0 A" -- actual welding current (displays during welding)
- **Bottom-right**: "0.0 V" -- actual voltage (displays during welding)
- **Bottom status bar**: Shows process "Stick", electrode type "70XX", electrode diameter "3/32\"", material thickness "16Ga", SETTINGS shortcut

Labels with arrows point to:
- **Electrode Diameter** indicator
- **Material Thickness** indicator
- **Process** indicator
- **Electrode Type** indicator
- **Hot Start** value (bottom-left, e.g., "6")
- **Arc Force** value (bottom-right, e.g., "0")

**WARNING! TO PREVENT SERIOUS INJURY: Welder is now energized and Open Circuit Voltage is present** when Right Knob is turned to ON.
