# Page 13: DCEN Polarity Setup + Gun Cable Connection

This page covers two critical setup topics: connecting the MIG gun cable to the wire feed mechanism, and setting up DCEN (Direct Current Electrode Negative) polarity for flux-cored gasless welding.

## Upper Section: Gun Cable Connector (Steps 13-15)

### Step 13-14: Wire Feed Mechanism Connection
Diagram shows the inside of the welder with labeled parts:
- **Knob** -- secures the gun cable connector to the wire feed mechanism
- **Gun Cable Connector** -- the end of the MIG gun cable that plugs into the wire feed mechanism socket
- **Wire Feed Mechanism** -- the assembly the connector plugs into

Two states are shown:
- **"Incorrect -- Connector not fully inserted"**: The connector is partially seated in the socket. This will cause gas leaks, preventing shielding gas from reaching the welding arc.
- **"Correct -- Connector fully inserted"**: The connector is fully seated with the knob tightened securely.

### Step 15: Wire Feed Control Cable
Diagram shows:
- **Wire Feed Control Cable** -- a separate cable from the MIG gun that carries control signals
- The cable routes through a hole on the Welder front and connects to the **Wire Feed Control Socket** inside the machine
- The plug fits the socket in **one specific orientation only**
- The lock ring on the cable plug must be tightened after insertion

## Lower Section: DCEN Flux-Cored (Gasless) Polarity Setup (Step 16)

### Title: "DCEN Flux-Cored (Gasless) Polarity Setup"

Large diagram showing the bottom front of the welder with the two power sockets and cable routing:

- **Wire Feed Power Cable** connects to the **Negative (-) Socket** (left socket as viewed from front)
- **Ground Clamp Cable** connects to the **Positive (+) Socket** (right socket as viewed from front)

The diagram clearly shows:
- The Wire Feed Power Cable coming from inside the welder, routed down to the Negative socket
- The Ground Clamp with its cable routed to the Positive socket
- Both cables twist clockwise all the way to lock in place
- Labels: "DCEN", "Wire Feed Power Cable in Negative Socket", "Ground Clamp Cable in Positive Socket"

## CRITICAL SAFETY NOTE:
This polarity (DCEN) is ONLY for flux-cored gasless welding. For solid core MIG welding (gas shielded), the polarity is reversed -- see page 14 for DCEP setup. Using the wrong polarity will result in poor weld quality, excessive spatter, and porosity.
